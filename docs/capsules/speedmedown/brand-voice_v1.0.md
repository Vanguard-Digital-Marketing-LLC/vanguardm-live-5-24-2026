# Capsule: brand-voice v1.0

Voice & tone for marketing copy and client-facing UI strings.

> Placeholder — fill from existing brand guidelines if/when they land in `docs/`.

## Working defaults
- **Direct over clever.** A clear sentence beats a punchy one if they conflict.
- **Operator language**, not corporate. "Your portal", not "the platform".
- **Specific over hedged.** "Ships in 7 days" beats "fast turnaround".
- Avoid emojis unless the surface is explicitly playful.
- Sentence case for headings; Title Case only for proper nouns and product names.

## Anti-patterns
- "Empower", "leverage", "seamless", "best-in-class" — banned.
- Faux-urgency CTAs ("Don't miss out!").
- Walls of marketing copy on dashboard surfaces — operators are there to work.
